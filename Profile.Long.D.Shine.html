☛ I can help you!

Statement

✦ While I don’t claim to be the foremost expert in this field, I am confident that I am uniquely positioned to provide you with the following FIVE things:

  1. ✔ I am willing to allocate 70% of my time to research your field of expertise and personal style, in order to create the most fitting and ideal product for you
  2. ✔ I am constantly seeking and acquiring new knowledge, consistently updating my work methods to provide the best experiences for each individual customer.
  3. ✔ I am not the type of person who works only 8 hours a day and then shuts down the computer. Going the extra mile to deliver the best results for clients brings me immense joy. After all, this job is truly my passion.
  4. ✔ Beyond visuals, I understand the importance of branding. I ensure that my designs reflect your values and messages accurately.
  5. ✔ I am ready to provide guidance to help you make informed decisions about what you want me to create. Additionally, I am open to listening and incorporating your feedback to enhance the quality of service.


Special

➤ Unlike other graphic designers who rush to complete designs solely based on your description or their subjective perspective to collect payment and then disappear, I take a different approach. If that’s all you need, you won’t have to seek me out, and I’m perfectly fine with that.

➤ However, if you want me to design for you, I invest time in understanding your field and your business. I provide tailored advice on the most ideal design styles for your individual needs and your business. Once we agree on the direction, I’ll begin working and deliver a product that will undoubtedly satisfy you.

  • ✓ I’ve discovered a truth in the market: your customers are more impressed by abstract and visually encoded information when they look at your media materials and office documents than by plain text.
  • ✓ I believe that overwhelming them with too much less relevant information is ineffective.
  • ✓ That’s why using symbolic imagery and visually represented content often yields the best results.


Adjuration

I’ll take care of the rest.

➤ If the final result doesn’t meet your expectations, there’s no need to pay me. I’m perfectly fine with that. I’ve never considered charging someone who isn’t satisfied with my work. Consider it a learning experience for future designs.
